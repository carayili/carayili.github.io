FAQ

How does the service work?

Once you book our service, our sugar painting artist will attend your event and offer 5–10 design options for children and guests to choose from.

Each sugar painting is freshly made on site as a handmade lollipop-style treat. We can usually create around 10–20 sugar paintings per hour, depending on whether the chosen designs are simple or more detailed.

How much does it cost?

Our sugar painting service starts from:

  • 1 hour — from £220
  • 2 hours — from £300
  • 3 hours — from £350

Additional travel fees may apply for locations in London Zone 3 and beyond. Extra charges may also apply for bookings on special festivals and holidays.

What do we need to provide?

You only need to provide one adult-sized table and chair at the event venue, and make sure there is a power socket nearby.

Our sugar painting artist will bring everything needed for the service, including a tablecloth, extension lead, cleaning supplies, and all sugar painting equipment.
