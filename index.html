About

Shanshan's Sugar Painting

London, UK

Tanghua (Sugar painting) is a traditional Chinese folk art made with warm melted sugar. The sugar is poured by hand onto a smooth surface to create delicate shapes such as animals, flowers, and lucky symbols.

Our main service is live sugar painting for private parties. We set up at your event, create sugar paintings in front of guests, and hand them out as sweet, handmade keepsakes.
